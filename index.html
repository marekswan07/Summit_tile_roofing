Welcome to Summit Tile Roofing

Providing the Willamette Valley with the Highest Quality Roofing Tile for over 50 years

50+ years ago, the Standley's started producing tile in a garage where they used a hand machine to trowel each tile one at a time. Today, 3 generations later, the Standley family operates Summit Tile roofing Inc. out of a 16,000 sq. foot facility where a state-of-the-art extruder is used. It’s attention to detail and quality has only improved and Summit Tile Roofing Inc. is known the world over for it’s high quality products.

Our tiles are made from a special mixture of Willamette Valley sand and cement combination that minimizes breakage. Whether you’re a homeowner or contractor, that means you save money. With less breakage, there are fewer tiles to replace and when we install them, we have the longest warranties in the roofing Industry.
